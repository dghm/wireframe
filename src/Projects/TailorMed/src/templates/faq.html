Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cold chain logistics services

Shipping Process

What temperature ranges do you support?

We support three main temperature ranges: Refrigerated (2-8°C) for standard pharmaceuticals and vaccines, Frozen (-20°C) for specialty products requiring deep freeze, and Ultra-Low (-80°C) for advanced therapies like cell and gene therapy products.

How do you ensure temperature stability during transit?

We use validated packaging systems with real-time temperature monitoring, insulated containers, and appropriate cooling agents (dry ice, liquid nitrogen vapor, or gel packs) depending on the temperature requirements. Our packaging is validated for specific time periods and transport conditions.

What documentation is provided with shipments?

Each shipment includes comprehensive documentation: temperature monitoring reports, chain of custody documentation, regulatory compliance certificates, customs documentation, and delivery confirmation. All documentation is available digitally through our tracking portal.

Tracking & Monitoring

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using our "Track Shipment" button in the top navigation, or by entering your tracking number on our tracking portal. We provide real-time updates on location, temperature conditions, and estimated delivery times.

What happens if there's a temperature excursion?

We have 24/7 monitoring systems that immediately alert our operations team and the client if any temperature excursion occurs. Our team will assess the situation, implement corrective actions, and provide detailed reports. We work closely with clients to determine the best course of action for affected products.

How often are temperature readings recorded?

Temperature readings are recorded every 15 minutes during transit, with immediate alerts for any excursions. For ultra-sensitive products, we can provide more frequent monitoring intervals as required.

Packaging & Storage

What packaging materials do you use?

We use a range of validated packaging systems including insulated containers, dry ice systems, liquid nitrogen vapor systems, and gel pack solutions. All packaging is validated for specific temperature ranges and transit times according to regulatory requirements.

How long can products maintain temperature stability?

Our packaging systems are validated for different time periods: Refrigerated (2-8°C) maintains stability for 72+ hours, Frozen (-20°C) for 96+ hours, and Ultra-Low (-80°C) for 120+ hours. Actual duration depends on ambient conditions and specific product requirements.

Do you provide packaging for small shipments?

Yes, we provide specialized packaging solutions for small shipments, including individual sample vials, clinical trial materials, and small batch quantities. Our packaging is scalable to accommodate various shipment sizes while maintaining temperature integrity.

Delivery & Customs

Do you handle customs clearance?

Yes, we provide comprehensive customs clearance services for international shipments. Our team handles all necessary documentation, regulatory compliance, and works with local customs authorities to ensure smooth clearance while maintaining cold chain integrity.

What are your delivery timeframes?

Delivery timeframes vary by destination and service level: Domestic deliveries typically take 1-3 days, international express 2-5 days, and standard international 5-10 days. For ultra-urgent shipments like cell and gene therapy products, we offer same-day and next-day delivery options.

Do you provide white-glove delivery services?

Yes, we offer white-glove delivery services for high-value and sensitive shipments. This includes direct delivery to specific locations, temperature-controlled handoff procedures, and specialized handling requirements as needed.

General Questions

What certifications do you hold?

We hold multiple industry certifications including GDP (Good Distribution Practice), GxP standards, ISO 9001:2015 quality management, and various regional pharmaceutical logistics certifications. We maintain regular audits and compliance reviews.

How do I get a quote for my shipment?

You can request a quote by clicking the "Get Quote" button in our navigation, filling out our contact form, or calling our customer service team. We'll need details about your shipment including origin, destination, temperature requirements, and product specifications.

Do you offer 24/7 customer support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 customer support for all shipments. Our emergency support line is available for urgent issues, and our operations team monitors all active shipments around the clock to ensure timely intervention if needed.
